Welcome to Meraki Platina

Where luxury meets lifestyle

Discover Meraki Platina, a prestigious gated community nestled in the heart of Chembur, Mumbai. What makes us truly exceptional is our unique blend of opulent amenities and a philosophy that goes far beyond mere housing – it's about embracing a vibrant and fulfilling lifestyle.

Crafted by Meraki Habitats LLP, a renowned real estate developer with an impressive 16-year track record of delivering exceptional projects, an unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction, and unmatched features.

Choose Meraki Platina as your new residence and embark on a journey that transcends the ordinary. Your path to a more luxurious and fulfilling lifestyle begins right here.
